### **The Commission Opportunity**

**Simple. Clear. Performance-Based.**

We’ve streamlined the compensation structure so you always know **exactly how you earn**.

### **Front-End Commissions (Immediate CPA)**

Whenever someone you refer purchases a **front-end product**, you earn a **flat $200 CPA per sale**.

Front-end products include:

- IAS
- Phantom Protocol

- This payout is:

- Fixed at $200 per sale
- Not tiered
- Not dependent on your mentorship level
- Automatically tracked for accuracy

If a front-end sale is qualified and not refunded, you earn $200—every time.

* * *

### **Back-End Mentorship Commissions (Longer-Term Opportunity)**

Back-end mentorship sales always pay out **30% total commission**.

How that 30% is distributed depends on **qualification**.

- **25% commission** goes to the first affiliate who is _qualified_ for the product sold
- **5% commission** goes to the unqualified seller or qualified upline as an override

Your **qualification level** is based on the highest mentorship tier you personally own:

- 3-Month
- 6-Month
- 12-Month
- Lifetime

You are qualified to earn the **full 25%** on:

- The product you own
- Any lower-tier product

If you are not qualified for the product sold, you may still earn **5%**, while the remaining 25% passes to the first qualified upline.

This structure rewards growth, progression, and long-term commitment—while keeping payouts consistent and fair.
