# COACHING RECRUITMENT

**Cherrington Media Is Recruiting Coaches — Build Your Business, Grow Your Skills, and Help Others Win**

This is your opportunity to step into a leadership role, share your expertise, and get paid — all while sharpening your own skills as an affiliate marketer.

We’re looking for motivated, results-driven coaches to join our team and work directly with mentees, guiding them toward success and helping them break through roadblocks.

**Why Become a Cherrington Coach?**

\*\*1\. Grow Your Own Business While Coaching\*\*

Teaching others keeps you sharp. You’ll reinforce your own skills, test strategies in real time, and stay ahead in the competitive affiliate marketing space.

\*\*2\. Earn from Private 1:1 Sessions\*\*

Book one-on-one coaching sessions with mentees and get paid for your expertise while making a real impact on their progress.

\*\*3\. Continued Access to Cherrington Tools\*\*

Even after your mentorship ends, you’ll maintain access to the Cherrington tools, resources, and systems that help you thrive.

\*\*4\. Unlimited Income Potential\*\*

The only cap on your earnings is the one you set for yourself. More sessions, more clients, more income — it’s that simple.

\*\*5\. Work With a Like-Minded Team\*\*

You’ll collaborate with driven professionals who share the same vision: creating success for ourselves and for the mentees we serve.

**What You’ll Do as a Coach**

\- Host private 1:1 coaching sessions.

\- Guide mentees through creatives, campaigns, and optimization strategies.

\- Help mentees find clarity, focus, and executable plans.

\- Motivate, inspire, and keep mentees accountable for results.

**Requirements to Apply**

\- Must have an \*\*active campaign running that is producing revenue\*\*.

\- Must possess \*\*strong knowledge of all Cherrington software and tools\*\*.

\- Must be able to clearly communicate strategies and mentor with patience and enthusiasm.

\- \*\*Coaches will be required to host 1–2 coaching calls per week\*\*
