# The Blitz™ (1.1):

# Master the TCE Approach to Affiliate Arbitrage

# Welcome to **The Blitz™**—your accelerated path to launching profitable affiliate arbitrage campaigns with focused daily actions that fit your schedule and budget. This intensive program takes our proven Build, Test, Scale framework and breaks it down into bite-sized tasks you can complete at your own pace.

# **By completing The Blitz™, you'll have:**

- Selected and joined profitable affiliate networks
- Created multiple banner ads and landing pages
- Launched live campaigns on proven traffic sources
- Completed $5,000 in systematic testing across 3 rounds
- Identified profitable campaigns ready to scale

# **Budget Breakdown:**

- **Round 1:** $1,500 (testing headlines & landing pages)
- **Round 2:** $500+ (testing images with winning headlines)
- **Round 3:** $3,000+ (testing multiple new placements)

# **No overwhelm. No unrealistic deadlines. Just focused action at your pace.**

# **Need help along the way?** We offer comprehensive support including live coaching calls 6 days/week, 24/7 community access, and done-for-you options through the TCE Concierge™. Technical issues? Our support team is here to help get you back on track quickly. [See full support details](https://experience.cherringtonmedia.com/welcome-to-the-blitz/\#tve-jump-19866abefc5).

While the MaxWeb portal will look different than ClickBank, the process of looking through the offers is similar. On the left hand menu in MaxWeb, click on “Campaigns” to see a list of products available to promote. You can then click on the image of the product for some basic info on the product. Some will have a VSL link in the description, while others you will need to apply to promote (by clicking the “Promote” button before receiving more information). This should not take more than 1 business day, and if you have any questions you can reach out to your Account Representative at MaxWeb, whose contact information will be on your MaxWeb home page (click “Dashboard” on the top left of your MaxWeb page to see their info).

ClickBank - Jump/Bridge Page Setup

**Definitions:**

**LANDING PAGE** \- any page the visitor "lands on" after clicking on an ad. These could be longer, story-based pages like an advertorial, or they could be very short "jump pages"

**BRIDGE PAGE** \- a type of landing page that serves as a "bridge" to another (usually longer) piece of copy

**JUMP PAGE** \-  a type of landing page and a type of bridge page that is typically very short, often used to get visitors to "jump" over and watch a VSL (Video Sales Letter). ("bridge page" and "jump page" will often be used interchangeably here.)

**VSL** \- "Video Sales Letter". This was quite literally a sales letter that had been converted to video format when first invented by Jon Benson. The text from the sales letter was displayed in black text on white slides. Now, a VSL is any video that sells a product.

**Process Overview for Creating Your Jump Page:**

To write the copy for your jump page, you'll first need to extract the text from the VSL (Video Sales Letter) for your chosen product.

This means downloading the video, using some sort of software or service to get a transcript from the video, and then cleaning it up so that the text is accurate.

Once you have a clean transcript from your VSL, you can then use that to come up with angles and write the copy for your jump pages using the bots and resources listed below.

## Publisher Overview - Know Your Options

## **You'll be working with one primary publisher during initial testing:**

- **Publisher #1: "Caterpillar"**

- **Native Advertising Platform** (no banners)
- **Placements:** Static Images (16x9, 9x16), GIFs (16x9, 9x16), Videos (16x9, 9x16)

- **ClickBank offers allowed**

- **Headlines/Visual uploaded separately** \-  "Native Advertising" means that you upload your headlines as plain text and your image or video files separately from the headlines

- **More flexible compliance**

**Note:** It's recommended that you begin testing using the Caterpillar publisher.

## **Secondary testing publishers:**

- **Publisher #2: "Grasshopper"**

- Banner Ads

- **Placements:** 970x250, 300x250, 970x550

- **ClickBank offers allowed**

- **Animated GIFs allowed** (best practice: use both animated & static in later image testing rounds)

- **More flexible compliance**

- **Higher CPCs** (but still profitable)

- **Publisher #3: "Crane"**

- Both Banner Ads and Native Ads Available
- **Banner Placements:** 970x250, 1536x864, 900x750, 1242x699

- **ClickBank offers NOT allowed** (Media Mavens only)

- **Animated GIFs NOT allowed** (static only)

- **Stricter Compliance**

- **Lower CPCs**

- **Important Considerations:**

- Choose your publisher based on your offer type (ClickBank = Caterpillar or Grasshopper only)

- All publishers offer high-quality traffic with profit potential

- You'll select ONE publisher and ONE placement for Round 1

- Plan your creative assets accordingly (animated vs. static)

**Note:** A fourth publisher ("Master") becomes available in the Scale Phase for dedicated emails only.

## Caterpillar - Understanding The Testing Reality

**Required Ad Budget: $5,000 minimum**

This budget covers systematic testing across multiple rounds:

- **Round 1: $500 minimum - Testing Headlines & Start Testing Landing Page Variations**

- Test 10 ad HEADLINES + 1 DESCRIPTION w/ 1 IMAGE (16x9)

- Test 25 landing page combinations (5 headlines × 5 hero shots)

- Typical return: -$400 (-80% ROI)

- **No Publisher Required Minimum:** Test to $500 minimum or 1x CPA (commission payout) per ad

- **Round 2: $600+ recommended - Testing Placements & Continue Landing Pages**

- **Test 6 placements** (2 IMAGES, 2 GIFs, 2 VIDEOS)  with 1 winning HEADLINE + DESCRIPTION from Round 1

**Convert your base image (16x9) from Round 1** and turn it into the 5 other placements --> 9x16 static image, GIFs in 16x9 & 9x16, videos in 16x9 & 9x16.

\*\*TIP: **Use Grok Imagine** to animate your static base image to create videos and GIFs.
- STATIC IMAGE 16x9
- STATIC IMAGE 9x16
- GIF 16x9
- GIF 9x16
- VIDEO 16x9
- VIDEO 9x16
- Continue testing landing page combinations, turn off worst performers
- Recommend at least 2x CPA (commission payout) per ad

- This means if your commission on the sale of a product is $50, you should plan to spend at least 2 x ($50 commission) x (6 placements) = $600
- Suggested: ~2x $CPA per placement tested

**Objective:** The goal of this round of testing is to find the most profitable placement for your offer. Poor performing placements can be turned off before reaching the 2x CPA spend level, however, in order to determine a true winner, you may need to take your top 2 or 3 placements well beyond the 2x CPA spend.

**WARNING:** If your WINNING placement is not returning a MINIMUM of 60% on your ad spend ($600 commissions on $1000 ad spend), you need to return to HEADLINE & DESCRIPTION TESTING before proceeding to Round 3.

Take your winning placement and do another set of 5 HEADLINES x 2 DESCRIPTIONS (total of 10 ads). Test to at least 1x CPA, recommended 2x CPA per ad to determine the winner, turning off losers early.

- **Round 3: $1000+ recommended - Test Winning Placement Variations & Continue Landing Pages**

- **Test 10 VISUAL CREATIVE VARIANTS on your winning placement** \- Take your winning ad from Round 2 and create 9 new images/videos to split test against it.
- For example, if your winning placement was a 9x16 GIF, then you should create 9 more GIFs in a 9x16 ratio to test.

- Use the same winning HEADLINE + DESCRIPTION you found from previous rounds on all 10 ads
- Continue testing landing page combinations, turn off worst performers
- Recommend at least 2x CPA (commission payout) per ad

- This means if your commission on the sale of a product is $50, you should plan to spend at least 2 x ($50 commission) x (10 ads) = $1000
- Suggested: ~2x $commission payout per ad tested

**Objective:** The goal of this round of testing is to find a new winning visual creative (image/gif/vid) for your offer. Poor performing ads can be turned off before reaching the 2x CPA spend level, however, in order to determine a true winner, you may need to take your top 2 or 3 ads well beyond the 2x CPA spend.

**HOW TO PROCEED:** If your WINNING ad is not returning a MINIMUM of 75% on your ad spend ($750 commissions on $1000 ad spend), you need to find your weak link in the chain and improve it. This could mean:

\- AD HEADLINE

\- AD DESCRIPTION

\- AD VISUAL CREATIVE

\- ADVERTORIAL HEADLINE

\- ADVERTORIAL HERO SHOT

\- ADVERTORIAL LEAD

Take your winning elements and do a test on variations of what you suspect is your weakest element. Test to statistical significance, turning off losers as significance is reached.

## Why This Specific Testing Sequence?

- **Round 1 ($500 minimum, ~$100 returned):**
- What You're Testing:

- 10 different HEADLINES
- 25 landing page combinations (5 headlines × 5 hero shots)

- **Why Round 1 Is The Hardest:**

- Headlines are the MOST CRUCIAL element for conversions
- You're testing the core messaging that makes or breaks your campaign
- This round typically has the highest losses (-80% ROI is normal)
- Finding winning headlines is the foundation everything else builds on
- Once you crack the headline code, Rounds 2 & 3 become much easier

Optimization Timeline:

- **$300 spent:** Optimize banner headlines (~$30/banner gives enough data)
- **$500 spent:** NOW choose your winning headline to move forward with

- **Success Criteria to Proceed to Round 2:**

- 1 WINNING HEADLINE
- If two are close in stats, choose one to move forward with to Round 2

What You'll Learn:

- Which placement connects with your audience
- Which landing page combinations convert
- Your baseline conversion rates

- **Round 2 ($600+ recommended, targeting 75% return):**

What You're Testing:

- 6 different placements IMAGES, GIFs, VIDEOS (paired with Round 1's winning headline + description)

\- Take your base image (16x9) from Round 1 and turn it into the 5 other placements --> 9x16 static image, GIFs in 16x9 & 9x16, videos in 16x9 & 9x16.

\*\*TIP: Use Grok Imagine to animate your static base image to create videos and GIFs.
- Continue testing landing page combinations from Round 1, weeding out the losers
- Recommended 2x CPA (commission payout) minimum spend per placement

Budget Recommendation:

- At least $600-1000 total (2x CPA (commission payout) per ad)

What You'll Learn:

- Which visual creative placement reaches your audience best
- How placement affects CTR and Conversion Rate
- Top landing page Headline/Hero variants

- **Round 2 Success Criteria:**

- Must see 60% return on ad spend ($600 commission on $1000 in ad spend)
- If criteria not met, return to HEADLINE + DESCRIPTION testing ( [see above](https://experience.cherringtonmedia.com/welcome-to-the-blitz/#tve-jump-19a02e534d9)) with your winning PLACEMENT

**After Round 2:** If you meet the success criteria, you're ready to move to Round 3 and test different visuals on your ads!

- **Round 3 ($1000+ recommended, targeting 80%+ return):**

What You're Testing:

- 10 different visuals on your winning placement (IMAGE, GIF, or VIDEO)

For example, if your winning placement was a 16x9 VIDEO, then you should create 9 more VIDEOS in a 16x9 ratio to test.
- Use your WINNING headline + description on all
- Continue testing landing page combinations from Round 1, weeding out the losers and narrowing in on your winning combination
- Recommended 2x CPA (commission payout) minimum spend per ad

Budget Recommendation:

- At least $600-1000 total (2x CPA (commission payout) per ad)

What You'll Learn:

- What visual imagery converts best
- How visual affects CTR and Conversion Rate
- Top landing page Headline/Hero variants

- **How to Proceed After Round 3:**

- Targeting at least a 75% return on ad spend ($750 commission on $1000 in ad spend)
- If criteria not met, find and fix your weak link ( [see above](https://experience.cherringtonmedia.com/welcome-to-the-blitz/#tve-jump-19a02f06720))

**After Round 3:** If you meet the success criteria, you're likely ready to vigorously test your DESCRIPTION, since that's the element that hasn't been given much attention yet.

Your Advertorial is also another big leverage point in your campaign performance. You may consider more Headline/Hero testing, or even testing new Leads (first section of body text), or a brand new Advertorial angle.

****GRASSHOPPER AND CRANE****

## GRASSHOPPER AND CRANE Understanding The Testing Reality

**Required Ad Budget: $5,000 minimum**

This budget covers systematic testing across multiple rounds:

- **Round 1: $1,500 minimum - Testing Headlines & Landing Pages**

- Test 20 banner ad HEADLINES

- Test 25 landing page combinations (5 headlines × 5 hero shots)

- Typical return: -$500 (-67% ROI)

- **Required: $1,500 minimum for initial placement**

- **Round 2: $500+ recommended - Testing Images & More Landing Pages**

- Test 20 banner ad IMAGES (with winning headlines from Round 1)
- Test 25 NEW landing page combinations
- **No minimum IF using same placement** (we recommend at least $500)
- Suggested: ~$25 per banner image tested

**Important:** Publisher partnerships require $1,500 minimum for any NEW placement. Once a placement is active, you can test additional creatives with any budget.

## Why This Specific Testing Sequence?

- **Round 1 ($1,500 minimum, ~$500 returned):** What You're Testing:

- 20 different banner HEADLINES
- 25 landing page combinations (5 headlines × 5 hero shots)

- **Why Round 1 Is The Hardest:**

- Headlines are the MOST CRUCIAL element for conversions
- You're testing the core messaging that makes or breaks your campaign
- This round typically has the highest losses (-80% ROI is normal)
- Finding winning headlines is the foundation everything else builds on
- Once you crack the headline code, Rounds 2 & 3 become much easier

Optimization Timeline:

- **$500 spent:** Optimize banner headlines (~$25/banner gives enough data)
- **$1,500 spent:** NOW optimize landing pages (analyze conversions & CTR to identify top 1-2 pages)

- **Success Criteria to Proceed to Round 2:**

- Minimum $300 in affiliate commissions (20% of spend)
- At least 2 profitable banner ads
- If you don't meet BOTH criteria, see " [What Happens After Round 1?](https://experience.cherringtonmedia.com/welcome-to-the-blitz/#tve-jump-198664b1412)" section

What You'll Learn:

- Which messages resonate with your audience
- Which landing page combinations convert
- Your baseline conversion rates
- Which angles completely fail

- **Round 2 ($500+ recommended, targeting break-even):** What You're Testing:

- 20 different banner IMAGES (paired with Round 1's winning headlines)
- 25 NEW landing page combinations
- Same placement = no minimum spend requirement

Budget Recommendation:

- At least $500 total (~$25 per banner image)
- Continue on same placement from Round 1
- No new placement fees

What You'll Learn:

- Which visuals grab attention
- How imagery affects CTR
- Refined landing page winners
- Your best complete banner ads (headline + image)

- **Round 2 Success Criteria:**

- Must see 2+ additional sales on $500+ spend
- Total campaign spend must reach $2,000 (Rounds 1 & 2 combined)
- If criteria not met, return to Round 1 options

**After Round 2:** If you meet the success criteria, you're ready to move to the SCALE PHASE!

## How to Monitor Your Campaign Performance

- **Daily Monitoring Schedule:**

- **Check once per day** to ensure traffic data is flowing through DIYTrax™

- **Update P&L Tracker™ daily** with your spend and revenue

- **Don't panic over daily fluctuations** \- focus on trends over time

- **Key Metrics to Track (In Order of Priority):**

- **Conversions**\- Total sales generated
- **Ad CTR** \- How well your banners attract clicks
- **Landing Page CTR** \- How well your pages engage visitors

- **When to Start Optimizing:**

**After $25 Spent Per Banner:**

- Begin pausing poor-performing ads once they have reached at least $25 in spend on that banner
- Focus on ads with the lowest:

- Conversion Rate (CR)
- Ad CTR
- Landing Page CTR

- For example, if an ad banner has spent $25 and has 33 ad clicks but 0 landing page clicks, make it inactive. This is a clear loser.

**After $500 In Total Spend:**

- After 5 ad banners have spent a full $80+ and turned inactive, you now have a baseline to compare against. Any banners reaching $25 in spend with metrics considerably lower than your completed 5 banners should be cut.

- Keep your best 10-12 ads running

- **After $750 Spent (Middle of Round 1):**

**If running 10 Angles test for ClickBank product:**

- Now you can begin to optimize landing pages by cutting the lowest performing
- Cut (turn inactive) the bottom performing 30% to 50% of your landing page angles
- Check again at the $1000 mark to potentially make more cuts

- **After $1,500 Spent (End of Round 1):**

**If running 5x5 Metric Mover test for Media Mavens product:**

- Now you can optimize landing pages
- Identify your top 1-2 performing pages
- Prepare for Round 2 decisions

**If running 10 Angles test for ClickBank product:**

- Identify your top 1-3 performing pages
- Prepare for Round 2 decisions

**\*Important:** Resist the urge to make changes before $25 in spend on a single banner or $500 in total campaign spend. You need sufficient data for meaningful optimization decisions.

## PHASE 3: SCALE - Multiplying Your Profits

**Prerequisites:** You can ONLY enter the Scale Phase when you have:

- Completed Rounds 1 & 2 of testing
- Identified profitable banner/landing page combinations
- Met the success criteria from Round 2

- **Scaling Method #1: Increase Budget on Winners**

The simplest way to scale:

- **Increase daily budget** on profitable placements
- **Increase CPC bids** to capture more traffic
- **Remove ALL non-profitable banners** from rotation
- Start with 2x budget, then 5x, then 10x as performance holds

**Example:** If you're profiting $50/day at $100 spend, test $200, then $500, then $1,000 daily budgets.

- **Scaling Method #2: Test New Placements**

Once you've maximized budget on your current placement:

**What You're Testing:**

- Your PROVEN banner combinations only
- Your PROVEN landing pages only
- NEW placements across publishers

**Publisher & Placement Options:**

- **Grasshopper:** 970x250, 300x250, 970x550
- **Crane:** 970x250, 1536x864, 900x750, 1242x699
- Test placements you didn't use in Rounds 1 & 2

**Budget Reality:**

- $1,500 minimum for EACH new placement
- Test 2-3 new placements = $3,000-$4,500
- This ensures statistical significance per placement

**What You'll Learn:**

- Which placements work for YOUR specific offers
- How different audiences respond to your winners
- Your profit margins across different traffic sources

- **Scaling Method #3: Graduate to "Master" Publisher**

**\*CRITICAL:** Only attempt Master after achieving consistent profitability on Caterpillar, Grasshopper or Crane!

**Master Publisher Details:**

- **Placement:** Dedicated email ONLY
- **Requirements:**

- Must show profitable history on other publishers
- Only your BEST performing creative assets
- Higher price point = higher bar for success

- **Why It's Different:**
