Onboarding walkthrough batch #4 — four fixes from the 6mo lane. Items 1+2 interact: ship together.

1. SMS/PHONE VALIDATION TRIANGLE: Phone is optional and SMS Notifications defaults checked — a member can save with SMS on and no phone, silently breaking the promised 1-hour text reminder. Fix on the Profile step: if SMS is checked and phone is empty, block save with an inline message on the phone field ("Add a phone number to receive text reminders — or uncheck SMS notifications"). Server-side: the profile save endpoint enforces the same rule (client validation is UX, server is the gate). Also handle the reverse path: any later surface that lets a member clear their phone while partnerCallSmsOptIn/SMS opt-ins are true must surface the same conflict. The send-point's skip-if-no-phone suppression stays as backstop. Decide-and-report: does an existing member row already have SMS-on + no-phone (the probes)? List them.

2. FIX THE BACK/RE-SAVE 400: PATCH onboarding step completion rejects re-saving an already-completed step ("Cannot complete step 2. Current step is 3"). Legitimate flow: member clicks Back to edit Profile, hits Save & Continue, gets a raw 400 and their edits' fate is unclear. Fix the advancement semantics: saving a step AT OR BEHIND the member's current step persists the form data and does NOT touch the step counter (idempotent revisit-save); only a save that would advance PAST current consults the sequential guard. The button on a revisited step should read/behave sanely (data saved, navigate forward to their actual current step). Confirm whether the member's edits were being persisted before the 400 or rejected wholesale — report which, and ensure post-fix the data always saves. Regression test: complete step 2 → advance to 3 → Back → edit a field → save → data updated, step still 3, no error.

3. CAP THE KICKOFF SLOT LIST AT 8: The merged coach-pool availability shows every slot all day (dozens of buttons). Show the FIRST 8 slots in the merged earliest-first order, then a "Show more times" expander revealing the rest for that day. Applies to both kickoff and partner-call booking grids. Keep the merged interleaving as-is under the cap.

4. TIMEZONE LABEL ON BOOKING PAGES: The "Times shown in your local timezone (America/New_York)" line still renders the raw IANA id. Use the same friendly label as the profile dropdown ("Eastern Time (ET)") — one shared label-for-IANA helper, applied to every surface that prints the member's zone.
