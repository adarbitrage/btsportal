# PILLAR \#1: THE BUSINESS MODEL

The first pillar of a successful digital business is **the business model**. This is the framework that your business operates within, the strategy that guides your actions, and the mechanism that generates your profits. It's the blueprint that shapes your business and determines its direction.

In the vast world of online business models, one stands out for its simplicity, predictability, and scalability: **Affiliate Marketing**. Over the past two decades, I've explored numerous online business models, but none have proven as consistently profitable as Affiliate Marketing, particularly when combined with paid media, a strategy also known as **Affiliate Arbitrage**.

So, what exactly is Affiliate Arbitrage? It's a simple yet powerful concept. It involves using paid advertising to promote affiliate offers, with the goal of earning more in affiliate commissions than you spend on the advertising. It's a delicate balancing act, a game of numbers where the aim is to tip the scales in your favor.

Let's break it down with an example. Suppose you're promoting a trendy gadget as an affiliate using Google Ads. The affiliate network you're working with pays you $60 for every gadget you sell. Your goal in Affiliate Arbitrage is to spend less than $60 on Google Ads to generate that sale. If you spend $40 on Google Ads to sell a gadget and earn a $60 commission, you've made a $20 profit. Now, imagine scaling that up. Selling 10 gadgets a day at that cost would net you a **$200 daily profi** **t**. Selling 1,000 gadgets a day would result in a staggering **$20,000 daily profit**. It's a model that's not only profitable but also highly scalable.

But the benefits of the Affiliate Arbitrage model don't stop there. Here are some additional perks:

- It eliminates the need to navigate complex platforms like Amazon, Etsy, Shopify, Clickfunnels, or WordPress. **We keep things simple and straightforward**.
- It doesn't require you to build a complete website. We're not in the business of building websites; **we're in the business of making profits**.
- It doesn't require you to create a product or service of your own. **We leverage existing products that already have a market demand**.
- It doesn't involve applying for merchant processing. **We bypass the red tape and get straight to business**.
- It frees you from dealing with customer support. **We focus on marketing, not customer service**.
- It doesn't require an existing audience base or following. **You can start from scratch and still turn a profit in your first week**.
- It allows you to track ROI in real time. As an affiliate, **you're paid on the front-end sale, giving you instant feedback on your performance**.

These are just a few of the many advantages of the Affiliate Arbitrage model. It's a tried-and-tested model that's been around since the dawn of the internet, built on the principles of Direct Response Marketing used by the world's wealthiest marketers. So, remember, when it comes to choosing a business model, Affiliate Arbitrage is the way to go.

**Why Boomers Love Trendy Gadgets**

# PILLAR \#4: THE TRAFFIC CHANNEL

As we venture into the fourth pillar of our strategy, we've already laid a solid foundation. We've identified our business model (Affiliate Marketing), our product (Trendy Gadgets), and our target demographic (Boomers). Now, it's time to address the question of WHERE we'll be promoting our products. The answer is **through the powerful medium of email**.

When we talk about email marketing, many people's minds immediately jump to the traditional method of collecting opt-in subscribers, offering them a free e-book or video course, and then bombarding them with daily follow-up emails in an attempt to make a sale. However, **our approach is going to be significantly different and more innovative**.

Our strategy revolves around leveraging the power of existing email lists. Instead of building our own list, we'll be seeking out those who already have extensive email lists and placing our ads within the emails they send out to their subscribers. This approach eliminates the need for list management, saving us from the hassle of collecting and storing emails in an autoresponder like Aweber or Mailchimp.

The beauty of this approach lies in its simplicity and effectiveness. We're essentially outsourcing the hard work of list building and management to others, while we focus on placing our ads in their emails. **Our primary goal is to master the art of converting advertising dollars into profit, and** **there's no better channel for this than email**.

Email list owners are always on the lookout for opportunities to monetize their lists, and they're more than happy to sell ad space in their emails. These ads can take various forms, from banner ads (also known as email sponsorships) to solo advertisements (known as dedicated emails).

Email traffic offers numerous advantages:

- **The scale is enormous**, thanks to the vast number of email lists available.
- Many newsletters are sent out daily, providing us with **plenty of inventory to purchase**.
- Some lists boast over a million subscribers and can **deliver our ads to these large audiences almost instantly**.
- Email list owners often derive most of their income from their lists, so they take good care of their subscribers. This means **our ads will be shown to responsive audiences**.
- Unlike platforms like Google or Facebook, email marketing **doesn't involve dealing with complicated, ever-changing algorithms**.
- Email list owners are generally more lenient about the types of ads we can run. **We don't have to worry about ads being rejected or accounts being disabled**.
- Email traffic is often warmer than other channels because the subscribers have already opted in to receive emails. This makes them **more receptive to the ads that come from the list owner**.
- **There's less competition in this channel** because most people are unaware of it. While everyone else is focusing on Google and Facebook, we'll be capitalizing on the untapped potential of email traffic.

In my experience, email marketing outperforms all other channels. I've tried everything from Native, Display, Programmatic, Social, Facebook Ads, Google Ads, Snapchat Ads, Instagram Ads, YouTube Ads, Twitter Ads, LinkedIn Ads, to Reddit Ads. **None of them have delivered the same level of scale and ROI as email**.

As part of your enrollment, you'll gain access to hundreds of underground list management companies, brokers, publishers, and networks that allow you to place ads within their newsletters. You'll never be left wondering where to buy advertising!

So, remember, in the world of online marketing, Email Traffic reigns supreme!
