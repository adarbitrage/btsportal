In this video, I'll show you how to generate landing page angles using the affiliate angle architect. With our phase two traffic source opening up, we have more opportunities to test lower dollar amounts per banner. This is a CPC traffic source, which means we pay for clicks rather than impressions. 

To clarify, CPM stands for cost per thousand impressions. You pay for your ad to be shown to a thousand people, and your cost per click is calculated based on how many clicks you receive from those impressions. In contrast, with CPC, you pay every time someone clicks on your ad. This shifts the focus from just getting clicks to ensuring that those clicks lead to strong engagement on your landing page and video sales letter.

As we transition to a CPC model, we can test landing pages in a new way. Up until now, you've received templates, but we're going to explore different angles for your landing pages to resonate better with your market. If you're struggling to gain traction or have hit a plateau, testing a new angle could be the key. This means reworking your landing page from top to bottom to align with a fresh perspective.

The templates we've provided have helped many mentees achieve click-through rates of 30% to 40% with strong headlines and hero shots. However, to stand out in affiliate marketing, we need to find unique angles that others aren't using. The affiliate angle architect is a tool designed to help you brainstorm these angles. It's important to note that this tool won't write your landing pages for you; it's meant for ideation.

When you start using the bot, you'll be prompted to enter details about your target market, such as age, gender, and location. For example, if you're targeting a weight loss offer, you might focus on women aged 45 and older in the United States. The bot will analyze your VSL transcript and provide various marketing angles tailored to your demographic.

Different demographics respond to different messaging, so it's crucial to input relevant details. The bot will generate a list of angles based on the information you provide. For instance, if you target a younger audience, you might receive angles that emphasize social media transformation. 

Once you input your details and VSL transcript, the bot will analyze the information and suggest angles that could resonate with your audience. You'll receive a range of options, and while some may be more effective than others, testing these angles can help you discover what works best for your market. 

This tool opens up new possibilities for testing and refining your landing pages, allowing you to quickly generate and evaluate multiple angles. Thank you for joining me, and I hope you find this tool helpful in your marketing efforts.
