In this video, I'll show you how to choose the right offer network for your affiliate arbitrage strategy. First, log into your portal and navigate to the Resources section. You'll find the affiliate networks option listed second. Click on that, and you'll see two choices: MediaMavens, which features our internal offers, and ClickBank, a well-established affiliate network that has been around for many years. While we are not affiliated with ClickBank, it is known for consistently paying out commissions to affiliates.

If you decide to go with ClickBank, simply click the Register button. If you're already registered, you can log in. Once logged in, you'll see their marketplace, which offers a variety of products across different niches, particularly in health. Typically, offers are sorted by gravity, from high to low. In the next video, I'll guide you on how to select an offer on ClickBank, but for now, just familiarize yourself with the platform.

For MediaMavens, click the Learn About MediaMavens button to access our dedicated page. Here, you can watch a video to learn more about the network and its perks. You'll also find a login button, and later, I'll show you how to set up your MediaMavens account for the first time. As you scroll down, you'll see all the MediaMavens offers. You can expand these categories to view different offers, which are regularly updated.

To explore an offer, click on a category, like beauty, and select View Offer. This will take you to the offer card for that product, where you can grab your affiliate link. Ultimately, you can choose to run offers from either MediaMavens or ClickBank. Remember, to access the affiliate networks, hover over Resources, click on the second tab, and make your selection.
