In this video, I'll show you how to find and choose a MediaMavens offer to promote. First, hover over the resources tab and select the second affiliate network, MediaMavens. Click the big green button that says "Learn More About MediaMavens." This will take you to the MediaMavens overview page, where you can watch a video and read about the perks of MediaMavens. You can also log in here.

To find an offer to promote, stay on this page and expand the categories to see the available offers. Keep in mind that new products are constantly being tested and added, so take some time to explore the list. You’ll see specific offer cards laid out below, but you don’t need to scroll through them all. If a name and description catch your interest, like "Slumber Seal, the wireless sleep symphony," click on "View Offer." This will scroll you down to the offer card, where you’ll find an image, an overall description, and a link that opens the sales page in a new tab. Here, you can view the affiliate commission, the consumer price, and grab your affiliate link, provided you've logged in and set up your MediaMavens account.

You can easily return to the top of the page by clicking the little arrow. As you review the products, pay attention to the varying commission rates and prices. Choose an offer that resonates with you, one that you feel comfortable promoting. Consider whether you fit the target demographic, know potential customers, or have familiarity with the market. There are many great options available, so select a product that appeals to you. In the next video, I’ll show you how to find your affiliate links for a MediaMavens offer.
