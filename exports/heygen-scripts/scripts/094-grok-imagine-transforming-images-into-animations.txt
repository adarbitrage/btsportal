In this video, I'll show you a cool tip to speed up your workflow. A couple of weeks ago, I discovered a tool that has been awesome for creating animated content. Let’s jump over to Google and search for under eye bags, a typical way to start a campaign. For example, let’s say we find an image of a lady with noticeable under eye bags. You can simply save that image to your desktop.

Next, head over to grok.com. For those who may not know, Grok is Elon Musk's AI platform, and I recommend the paid plan, which is about twenty dollars a month. It's definitely worth it. One of the features I want to highlight is called Imagine. You can find it in the left sidebar. Click on Imagine and upload the image you just downloaded. Once you double-click the image, it will begin processing. 

What’s really impressive is that Grok Imagine creates a video from any image you upload. It even adds audio, which you might not want, but it’s a fantastic way to turn a static image into an animation quickly. You can download the animation or choose to redo it with different options. I usually stick with the normal setting, but you can also try the fun option, which can get a bit wild. 

After processing, you can see the results. Some animations might be usable, while others may not be what you expected. I also tried the spicy option, but it prompted an age confirmation, indicating it might include adult content. So, I recommend sticking to the normal or fun settings. Grok Imagine is a great tool for creating animations that you can use in ads, banners, or on any part of your landing page. That’s the tip for today!
