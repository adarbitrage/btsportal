In this video, I'll show you how to set up your landing page using Skeeter Strike as an example. I want to clarify that this is not a recommendation to promote Skeeter Strike. It's simply the product I've chosen for this demonstration. Please select a product that resonates with you, keeping in mind that some items are seasonal. For instance, running a Skeeter Strike campaign in winter may not be effective due to the lack of mosquitoes. Do your own research and choose a product based on your instincts and experiences.

Now, let’s look at our setup. We have our e-commerce product page, which is the final step in our funnel where the sales transaction occurs. A funnel is a series of steps a customer takes online to make a purchase or complete another desired action. Since the e-commerce product page is out of our control, our task is to create a landing page that effectively connects with this sales mechanism. 

We need to ensure our messaging aligns with the product page, directing potential customers in a way that prepares them to make a purchase. This is where our landing page comes in. We will move one step back in the funnel to maintain congruity between the e-commerce product page, our sales mechanism, and our advertorial, which serves as a pre-sales tool to warm up and frame the offer for the customer. 

Next, we will set up our advertorial in Flexy. You'll need to configure the basic settings of your Flexy account first, and then we’ll proceed to create the specific page and advertorial.
