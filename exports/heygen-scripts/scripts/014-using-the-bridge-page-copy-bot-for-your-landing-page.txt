Title: Using the Bridge Page Copy Bot for Your Landing Page
Original Title: CB LP - How to Use the Bridge Page Copy Bot
Video ID: tNlRT6KKBvXc47iq

---

In this video, I'll show you how to get copy for your landing page, also known as a bridge page or jump page. These short landing pages direct people to a ClickBank VSL. We'll use the bridge page copy bot along with the angles generated by the affiliate angle architect bot. 

First, open the bridge page copy bot in Poe. You'll also need your affiliate angle architect conversation open because the bridge page copy bot will ask for demographics. You’ll need to provide basic information like age, gender, and geographic location. It’s a simple process, so just fill that in. Next, you need to provide the transcript from the VSL. You can either copy and paste the text or drag and drop a .txt file with the transcript into the bot.

Now, let’s move on to the angles. Head back to the angle architect and select your top angle. Highlight angle number one, copy it, and then paste it into the bridge page copy bot. The bot will start writing your landing page for you. 

The result is a concise jump page with strong copy that encourages viewers to click through and watch the video. It maintains the same angle and headline, such as "toxic liver sludge making you gain weight while you sleep." The subheadline, "Mayo Clinic study exposes why 100% of overweight Americans have this hidden liver damage and the 10-second red wine trick that flushes it out for rapid fat loss," is also preserved. 

The body copy begins with a compelling question, drawing readers in. It explains the shocking discovery by Mayo Clinic researchers about toxic sludge clogging the liver and how it affects weight gain. It highlights a simple 10-second morning ritual that can help dissolve this sludge and boost metabolism. 

Once you have your first jump page, you can repeat the process for additional angles. Just grab the next angle, paste it in, and let the bot generate the copy. To extract the copy, click on the three dots at the top for more actions, then select "copy message." This allows you to paste the formatted text into a Google Doc, Word document, or any text editor. 

That’s how you use the bridge page copy bot to create effective copy for your jump pages using angles from the affiliate angle architect bot.
