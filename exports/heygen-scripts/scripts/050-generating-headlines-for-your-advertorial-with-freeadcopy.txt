Title: Generating Headlines for Your Advertorial with FreeAdCopy
Original Title: Generate Advertorial Headlines with FreeAdCopy
Video ID: 9880wmsSJBKP1WRk

---

In this video, I'll show you how to generate headlines for your advertorial using the free AdCopy.com app. Once you're in your free AdCopy account, go to the templates section on the left and click on the miscellaneous card. From there, select the Halbert headline generator. 

You’ll need to fill out some information, but you can create form data from a URL. Just grab the URL from your advertorial, paste it in, and hit submit. This will scan the site and extract the essentials. After a moment, it will generate the form for you. You can adjust or modify the details if you want, but I’ll stick with the defaults and click generate ad. You’ll see the headlines appear on the left side quickly.

Now you have 40 headlines to choose from. Some examples include: "MIT graduate unveils the lantern that ends mosquito madness," "How families conquered mosquitoes without a single chemical," and "Finally, a mosquito solution safe for kids and pets." You can download, copy, or edit these headlines as needed.

If you want to learn more about writing effective headlines, go to the learning section on the left. Search for "headline" and look for the training on the biggest headline mistakes to avoid. This training features insights from experts Stefan Georgi and Justin Goff, who provide valuable suggestions for improving your headlines.

Remember, great headlines often include elements like curiosity, addressing pain points, promising solutions, and maintaining simplicity. While not every headline will have all these components, incorporating as many as possible will strengthen your headlines. 

That's it! You can use the templates and the Halbert generator on freeadcopy.com to create compelling headlines for your advertorial. Once generated, you can find them in the "my ads" section to continue working with your 40 headlines.
