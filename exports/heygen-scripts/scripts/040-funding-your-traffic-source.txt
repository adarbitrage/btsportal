In this video, I'll walk you through funding your account so you're ready to run your campaign. You should have already set your traffic information, saved the form, and uploaded your ad banners. Now, go to the upper right-hand corner to find the appropriate traffic source. At the time of this recording, that’s the Responsive Rolodex Phase 2. This is the traffic source we want to run, and it should be selected under your basic info when you set up your campaign. 

We are phasing out the Phase 1 Responsive Rolodex traffic source, so the names may change in the future. For now, make sure you're using the Responsive Rolodex Phase 2. Whichever traffic source you're running, ensure it matches what you've selected. To fund your account, click on the fund button. This will open a new page where you'll see the Responsive Rolodex Test Phase Rounds 3 and 4 Email Sponsorships CPC Bidding. 

Click the fund button next to the correct option for the Phase 2 traffic source, which is CPC Email Sponsorships. Scroll down to enter your funding amount, which needs to be at least $1,500, probably a bit more for your first round of testing. Then, click Add to Cart. I won't complete this checkout process since I'm in a dummy account, but you would click Proceed to Checkout and continue with the process to fund your account. 

After funding, if you paid with a card, you should see your balance reflected almost immediately. If you don’t see it right away, check back in half an hour to an hour. If it hasn’t shown up after an hour, please reach out to support at BTSmedia.com. Let them know you've funded your account but the balance isn’t reflected. They’ll assist you. Don’t worry; the money hasn’t disappeared. If you made the payment, we have a record of it and can apply that balance to your account. If you pay by card, the balance should show up almost instantly. If you use an account transfer or wire, it may take longer. If you don’t see it within an hour, contact support to update your balance. Once your account is funded with more than $1,500, you’ll be ready to submit your banners and run your campaign. We'll cover that in a later video.
