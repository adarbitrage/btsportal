Title: Submitting Your Ad Banners and Activating Your Campaign
Original Title: DIYTRAX Traffic 6 - Submit Ad Banners and Turn Campaign Toggle to Active

---

In this video, I'll show you how to submit your ad banners and activate your campaign. First, ensure that everything looks right after your final check. Then, navigate to the traffic source tab. You might want to adjust your CPC bid to increase your share of the traffic. Starting with a bid higher than 75 cents, perhaps around a dollar, can be effective. You can choose to gradually increase your bid until you achieve the desired traffic flow or start higher and lower the cost per click based on your results.

The traffic you receive will depend on your bid for different ad placements and the effectiveness of your marketing materials. A higher click-through rate means more clicks, which will help you secure a larger share of the traffic. Stronger marketing materials can attract traffic even with a lower cost per click bid. Conversely, if your ad banners are less effective, you may need to set a higher bid to gain visibility.

Once your banners are ready and you’re confident they meet your standards, click the submit button. Remember, you won’t be able to make changes after this point, so double-check everything before submitting. After submission, you should see a confirmation that your traffic data has been submitted successfully. Some options will gray out and lock, indicating that changes can no longer be made. Your campaign will be pending review and will go live within 72 hours.

Now, return to the basic info tab. After submission, you’ll notice that your tab has turned active, meaning your campaign is now live. Make sure you are completely ready before submitting your ad banners on the traffic source page. Now, all that's left to do is wait for the review and for your campaign to go live.
