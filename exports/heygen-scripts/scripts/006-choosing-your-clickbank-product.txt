In this video, I'll show you how to find and select a top offer on ClickBank to promote. We'll focus on the technical steps rather than the philosophy behind choosing a product. When you're logged into your ClickBank account, look for the Affiliate Marketplace on the left sidebar. Click that link, and you'll be taken to a page where you can select Top Offers. 

You might notice that there are two different links for Top Offers, and they can display different sets of products. One link might show Mitalin at the top, while the other might list PrimeBiome first. Typically, the link that ranks offers by gravity score will give you the highest-ranking products. For example, you might see Mitalin, PrimeBiome, and Prostovive among the top offers. 

Another way to access top offers is by searching for the ClickBank affiliate marketplace. I recommend going to ClickBank.com/affiliates. Once there, hover over the affiliates tab and select ClickBank Top Offers. This will provide a recent rundown of the best offers, categorized by type, such as supplements or weight loss. For April 2025, PrimeBiome is ranked number one, followed by Sumatra Slim Belly and AquaSculpt. Any of these would be excellent choices to promote.

You can also use CBSnooper.com to analyze these products further. By searching for a specific product like PrimeBiome, you can access various statistics, including gravity scores and sales trends. While the free version has some ads, it still provides valuable insights into how products are performing. For instance, you can see if a product is gaining traction or if its gravity score is fluctuating. 

Once you've narrowed down your options, like PrimeBiome, check out the affiliate page for that product. Look through the affiliate resources, get your hop link, review the video sales letter, and understand any traffic restrictions. This will help ensure that you choose a product that aligns with your promotional strategies. That's how you find and choose an offer on ClickBank.
