In this video, I'll show you how to log into MediaMavens for the first time and set up your account to find and promote your offers. Start by going to the resources section. The second tab down is affiliate networks. Click on that, and it will open up your affiliate networks, where you'll see MediaMavens listed. Click on the green "Learn About MediaMavens" button, and you'll be welcomed to MediaMavens. 

At the top, you can watch a video to learn about your account. If you scroll down a bit, you'll see some of the perks of MediaMavens, followed by a list of offers available to you. Each offer displays the commission details, and clicking on any of them will take you to more information. 

Now, going back up, you'll find a big green login button at the bottom of the perks list. Click that to access MediaMavens. Once it opens, fill in your first name, last name, email, time zone, and language. Make sure to accept the cookies, then click next. On the following page, enter your address and click next again. Additional information is optional, so you can simply click finish. 

Now you're in your MediaMavens portal. You won't have to complete this process again. After your first login, the next time you click the login button, it will take you directly to the main dashboard. In the next video, I'll show you where to find your offers and links.
