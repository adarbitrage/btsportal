Description: Learn how to promote BTS offers using Meta and Google Ads while building real marketing skills and earning performance-based compensation

Content:

Overview

Build Test Scale offers qualified members the opportunity to promote BTS offers using Meta and Google Ads—two of the most powerful traffic platforms available. This initiative allows you to build transferable marketing skills while earning performance-based compensation.

By participating in this program, you can:

- Build hands-on media buying experience applicable beyond BTS
- Learn campaign setup, testing, and optimization strategies
- Reach individuals who genuinely need these programs
- Earn performance-based compensation while promoting offers you believe in

Whether your goal is skill-building, additional income, or both, this program allows you to achieve both simultaneously.

Commission Structure

We have streamlined the compensation structure to provide clarity on all earnings.

Front-End Commissions (Immediate CPA)

You earn a flat $200 CPA per sale whenever someone you refer purchases a front-end product.

Front-end products include:

- IAS
- Phantom Protocol

This payout is:

- Fixed at $200 per sale
- Not tiered
- Not dependent on your mentorship level
- Automatically tracked for accuracy

If a front-end sale is qualified and not refunded, you earn $200 per transaction.

Back-End Mentorship Commissions (Long-Term Opportunity)

Back-end mentorship sales pay out 30% total commission. The distribution of this 30% depends on your qualification level.

Commission Distribution:

- 25% commission goes to the first affiliate who is qualified for the product sold
- 5% commission goes to the unqualified seller or the qualified upline as an override

Qualification Levels:

Your qualification level is based on the highest mentorship tier you personally own:

- 3-Month Mentorship
- 6-Month Mentorship
- 12-Month Mentorship
- Lifetime Mentorship

Earning Structure:

You are qualified to earn the full 25% commission on:

- The mentorship product tier you personally own
- Any lower-tier mentorship product

If you are not qualified for the product sold, you may still earn 5%, while the remaining 25% passes to the first qualified upline affiliate.

This structure rewards growth, progression, and long-term commitment while maintaining consistent and fair payouts.

Commission Payouts & Tracking

Payment Schedule:

Commissions are reconciled and paid every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

All sales and commissions are fully trackable using your CAP account.
