# Logging Into MediaMavens for the First Time

**Category:** platform_guide  
**Topics:** MediaMavens, account setup, affiliate networks  

## Setting Up Your MediaMavens Account  

MediaMavens is an affiliate network platform where you can find and promote offers. Follow these steps to log in and set up your account for the first time.  

### Step-by-Step Instructions  

1. **Access MediaMavens**  
   - Navigate to the "Resources" section in your BTS dashboard.  
   - Click on the second tab labeled "Affiliate Networks."  
   - Locate MediaMavens and click the green "Learn About MediaMavens" button.  

2. **Explore MediaMavens Overview**  
   - On the MediaMavens page, you can watch an introductory video to learn more about the platform.  
   - Scroll down to view the perks of using MediaMavens and a list of available offers.  
   - Clicking on any offer will display more details about it.  

3. **Log Into MediaMavens**  
   - Scroll to the bottom of the perks list and click the large green "Login" button.  

4. **Complete the Onboarding Process**  
   - Fill in the required fields:  
     - First Name  
     - Last Name  
     - Email  
     - Time Zone  
     - Language  
   - Accept cookies if prompted, then click "Next."  
   - On the next page, enter your address and click "Next."  
   - Additional information is optional. You can skip this step by clicking "Finish."  

5. **Access Your MediaMavens Portal**  
   - Once the onboarding process is complete, you will be directed to the MediaMavens dashboard.  
   - For future logins, clicking the "Login" button will take you directly to the dashboard without repeating the setup process.  

## Key Takeaways  

- MediaMavens is accessible via the "Affiliate Networks" tab in the BTS Resources section.  
- The onboarding process requires basic personal information but skips optional fields if desired.  
- After the first login, subsequent access to MediaMavens will be seamless, taking you directly to the dashboard.  
- The platform provides an overview of available offers and their commissions, helping you choose what to promote.

# Overview of Affiliate Arbitrage Process

**Category:** strategy  
**Topics:** affiliate arbitrage, traffic sources, landing pages, media mavens, ClickBank, advertorials, commissions  

## What is Affiliate Arbitrage?

Affiliate arbitrage is a marketing strategy where you earn a profit by driving paid traffic to affiliate offers. The process involves:  
1. Running ads on a traffic source.  
2. Sending clicks from those ads to a landing page you control.  
3. Redirecting clicks from the landing page to the offer owner’s sales page.  
4. Earning commissions when a purchase is made.  

Your profit is the difference between the cost of your ads and the commissions you earn:  
**Profit = Commissions – Ad Spend.**

## The BTS Affiliate Arbitrage Method

The BTS method uses two primary affiliate networks: **Media Mavens** and **ClickBank**. Each has unique characteristics and processes.

### Media Mavens Network

1. **Traffic Sources:**  
   - Email newsletters.  
   - Display advertising networks (e.g., banner ads).  

2. **Process:**  
   - Ads direct traffic to an **advertorial** (a blend of advertisement and editorial content).  
   - The advertorial links to an **e-commerce product page**.  
   - When a purchase is made, you earn a commission.  

3. **Advantages:**  
   - Proven advertorial templates provided.  
   - Flat CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) payouts with no clawbacks, chargebacks, or refunds deducted.  
   - Exclusive to BTS program members, reducing competition.  

### ClickBank Network

1. **Traffic Sources:**  
   - Display ads.  
   - Native advertising within ClickBank-supported networks.  

2. **Process:**  
   - Ads direct traffic to a **jump page** or **bridge page** (a brief landing page).  
   - The jump page links to a **Video Sales Letter (VSL)** page.  
   - The VSL sells the product, and commissions are earned when a purchase is made.  

3. **Considerations:**  
   - Requires you to create your own landing pages.  
   - Revenue share model: commissions are subject to clawbacks for refunds or chargebacks.  
   - Open to all affiliates, leading to higher competition.  

## Choosing Your Starting Point

- **Media Mavens:** Recommended for beginners due to its lower setup complexity, exclusive access, and flat CPA payouts.  
- **ClickBank:** Suitable for those with experience in creating landing pages and managing refund risks.  

## General Process Overview

1. **Set Up Ads:**  
   - Use display banners or native ads on one or more traffic sources.  

2. **Drive Traffic to a Landing Page:**  
   - Media Mavens: Use a provided advertorial.  
   - ClickBank: Create a jump page or bridge page.  

3. **Redirect to the Sales Page:**  
   - Media Mavens: E-commerce product page.  
   - ClickBank: Video Sales Letter (VSL) page.  

4. **Earn Commissions:**  
   - Commissions are earned when a purchase is made on the sales page.  

## Key Takeaways

- Affiliate arbitrage profits come from the difference between ad spend and commissions.  
- The BTS method offers two networks: Media Mavens (exclusive, CPA model) and ClickBank (open, revenue share model).  
- Media Mavens is beginner-friendly with provided advertorials and no clawbacks.  
- ClickBank requires more setup and carries refund risks but offers broader access.  
- The process involves setting up ads, driving traffic to a landing page, and earning commissions from sales.

# Choosing Your Offer Network

**Category:** strategy  
**Topics:** affiliate networks, ClickBank, MediaMavens, offer selection  

## Overview of Offer Networks

The first step in affiliate arbitrage is selecting the offer network you want to work with. At Build Test Scale (BTS), you have two primary options: **MediaMavens** and **ClickBank**. Each network has unique features, and your choice will depend on your goals and preferences.

### MediaMavens

MediaMavens is BTS's internal affiliate network. It offers exclusive perks and a curated selection of offers. Here's how to navigate it:

1. **Access MediaMavens**:  
   - Go to the "Resources" section in your portal.  
   - Select "Affiliate Networks" and click on "Learn About MediaMavens."  

2. **Explore Offers**:  
   - Scroll through the categories to view available offers.  
   - Categories and offers are updated frequently, with new options added regularly.  

3. **View Offer Details**:  
   - Click on a category (e.g., Beauty) to expand the list of offers.  
   - Select "View Offer" to see the offer card, which includes details about the product and your affiliate link.  

4. **Set Up Your Account**:  
   - If you’re new to MediaMavens, you’ll need to set up your account. A later training module will cover this process in detail.  

### ClickBank

ClickBank is a well-established third-party affiliate network with a wide variety of offers across multiple niches. It has been a trusted platform for many years and is known for its reliable commission payouts.

1. **Access ClickBank**:  
   - Navigate to the "Affiliate Networks" section in your portal.  
   - Click on "Register" to create an account or log in if you already have one.  

2. **Explore the Marketplace**:  
   - The ClickBank marketplace features offers in various niches, with a focus on categories like health.  
   - Sort offers by "Gravity" (high to low) to identify top-performing products. Gravity is a metric that indicates how well an offer is converting for affiliates.  

3. **Choose an Offer**:  
   - Browse the marketplace to find offers that align with your target audience and marketing strategy.  
   - A detailed guide on selecting offers from ClickBank will be provided in the next training module.  

## How to Access Affiliate Networks

1. Log in to your BTS portal.  
2. Hover over the "Resources" tab.  
3. Select "Affiliate Networks" (second option).  
4. Choose between MediaMavens and ClickBank based on your preferences.  

## Key Takeaways

- **MediaMavens** is BTS's internal network, offering exclusive, curated offers with frequent updates.  
- **ClickBank** is a third-party network with a long-standing reputation and a wide range of offers across niches.  
- To access either network, navigate to the "Affiliate Networks" section under "Resources" in your BTS portal.  
- Gravity is a key metric on ClickBank to identify high-performing offers.  
- A later module will provide step-by-step instructions for setting up accounts and selecting offers on both platforms.
